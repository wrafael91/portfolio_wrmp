About Me

Looking for new challenges

My name is William Manchabajoy, I'm 30 years old, I was born in Cali, Colombia. I like to read and to do some sport. I'm getting started on web development, although, I've spent some years in university studying engineering, but it really helped me to realize the amazing opportunity both technology and software development is about. That's the reason why I signed up in a bootcamp in 'Make It Real', a startup company in which I've learned programming languages like HTML, CSS and Javascript, among others.

I like the most to discover new things and solving out problems, therefore, by doing coding and interacting on different technologies I've a got a better understanding on how things are going on each day and what is the role we play and what is society demanding on current time.
